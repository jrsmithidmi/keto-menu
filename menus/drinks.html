Keto Friendly Drinks

1. Bulletproof Coffee

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 15-20g | Protein: ~1g | Calories: ~180-220

Ingredients:

  • Coffee
  • Grass-fed butter
  • MCT oil (or coconut oil)

A morning staple: coffee blended with grass-fed butter and MCT oil for sustained energy.

2. Unsweetened Iced Tea

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: 0

Ingredients:

  • Black or green tea
  • Optional lemon juice
  • Sugar-free sweetener (optional)

Stay refreshed with black or green tea poured over ice. Add lemon juice or a keto-friendly sweetener to taste.

3. Sparkling Water

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: 0

Ingredients:

  • Carbonated water
  • Optional zero-calorie flavoring

Carbonated water in plain or zero-calorie flavored varieties is a great soda substitute.

4. Coconut Milk Smoothie

Carbs: 4-5g | Fat: 10-15g | Protein: ~2g | Calories: ~150-200

Ingredients:

  • Coconut milk
  • Spinach
  • Sugar-free sweetener (optional)

Blend coconut milk, spinach, and optional sugar-free sweetener for a creamy, keto-friendly green smoothie.

5. Bone Broth

Carbs: 1-2g | Fat: 3g | Protein: 6-9g | Calories: ~50-80 (per cup)

Ingredients:

  • Bones (chicken, beef, etc.)
  • Water
  • Herbs & spices

A savory drink that provides collagen, vitamins, and minerals. Great for electrolytes.

6. Unsweetened Almond Milk

Carbs: 1-2g | Fat: 2-3g | Protein: 1g | Calories: ~30-40 (per cup)

Ingredients:

  • Unsweetened almond milk

A popular milk alternative with fewer carbs than dairy milk. Perfect for smoothies or coffee.

7. Low-Carb Protein Shake

Carbs: 3-5g | Fat: 5-10g | Protein: 20-25g | Calories: ~150-200

Ingredients:

  • Keto-friendly protein powder
  • Water or almond milk
  • Optional MCT oil or nut butter

Use a keto-friendly protein powder with water or almond milk for a quick meal replacement.

8. Matcha Latte (Keto Style)

Carbs: 1-2g | Fat: 5-6g | Protein: 2g | Calories: ~60-80

Ingredients:

  • Matcha powder
  • Unsweetened almond or coconut milk
  • Optional MCT oil

Blend matcha powder with unsweetened almond milk or coconut milk, plus a dash of MCT oil.

9. Herbal Teas

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: 0

Ingredients:

  • Herbal tea bag or loose leaf (mint, chamomile, etc.)

Mint, chamomile, and other unsweetened herbal teas provide calming, flavorful hydration.

10. Infused Water

Carbs: ~1g (depends on fruit/herbs) | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~5-10

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Slices of cucumber, lemon, or berries

Add slices of cucumber, lemon, or berries to water for a refreshing, low-carb twist.

11. Keto Hot Chocolate

Carbs: 2-3g | Fat: 10g | Protein: 2g | Calories: ~120

Ingredients:

  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Heavy cream
  • Keto sweetener

Use unsweetened cocoa, heavy cream, and a keto sweetener for a rich chocolatey treat.

12. Electrolyte Drink

Carbs: 0-2g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~0-10

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Sugar-free electrolyte powder or salt

Add sugar-free electrolyte powders or salt to water to stay hydrated on keto.

13. Kombucha (Low-Sugar)

Carbs: 2-4g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~30-50 (per 8 oz)

Ingredients:

  • Low-sugar kombucha

Look for brands with under 5g net carbs and unsweetened or partially fermented versions.

14. Keto Iced Coffee

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 2-5g | Protein: 1g | Calories: ~30-60

Ingredients:

  • Brewed coffee (chilled)
  • Unsweetened almond milk
  • Sugar-free sweetener

Chill brewed coffee, add unsweetened almond milk and sugar-free sweetener for a quick refresher.

15. Chia Fresca

Carbs: 3-4g | Fat: 2-3g | Protein: 1-2g | Calories: ~40-50

Ingredients:

  • Chia seeds
  • Water
  • Lemon/lime juice
  • Sugar-free sweetener (optional)

Mix chia seeds into water with lemon/lime juice and sweetener. Let it sit until it gels slightly.

16. Sugar-Free Lemonade

Carbs: 1-2g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~10

Ingredients:

  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Keto sweetener

Combine water, lemon juice, and a sugar-free sweetener. Serve over ice for a classic summer drink.

17. Low-Carb Michelada (Adult Beverage)

Carbs: ~3-4g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~50-80

Ingredients:

  • Low-carb beer
  • Hot sauce
  • Lime juice, salt

Use a low-carb beer, hot sauce, lime juice, and salt. Keep an eye on carbs from beer brand choice.

18. Vodka Soda (Adult Beverage)

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~100

Ingredients:

  • Plain vodka
  • Sparkling water
  • Twist of lemon or lime

Plain vodka with sparkling water and a twist of lemon or lime is low-carb. Consume alcohol in moderation.

19. Ginger Turmeric Tea

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~5

Ingredients:

  • Fresh ginger
  • Turmeric root
  • Water
  • Keto sweetener (optional)

Boil sliced ginger and turmeric root for a warming, anti-inflammatory beverage. Sweeten if needed.

20. Keto Horchata

Carbs: 3-4g | Fat: 6g | Protein: 1-2g | Calories: ~80

Ingredients:

  • Almond milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Keto sweetener
  • Heavy cream or coconut cream (optional)

Almond milk, cinnamon, and a keto sweetener blended with a touch of heavy cream or coconut cream.
