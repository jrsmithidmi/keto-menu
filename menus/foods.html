Keto Friendly Foods

1. Avocado

Carbs: ~2g net | Fat: 15g | Protein: 2g | Calories: ~160 (per 100g)

Ingredients:

  • Fresh avocado

A creamy, nutrient-dense fruit packed with healthy monounsaturated fats and fiber.

2. Eggs

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 5g | Protein: 6g | Calories: ~77 (per egg)

Ingredients:

  • Whole eggs (any style)

A versatile and affordable source of protein and healthy fats, perfect for any meal.

3. Leafy Greens (Spinach, Kale)

Carbs: 1-2g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 2-3g | Calories: ~20-30 (per cup)

Ingredients:

  • Spinach
  • Kale
  • Other leafy greens

Low-carb, high-vitamin vegetables that add volume and nutrition to any keto meal.

4. Salmon

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 7g | Protein: 20g | Calories: ~180 (per 3 oz)

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillet

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids and a high-quality protein source.

5. Cheese (Cheddar, Mozzarella)

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 9g | Protein: 7g | Calories: ~110 (per 1 oz)

Ingredients:

  • Cheese of choice (Cheddar, Mozzarella, etc.)

A delicious dairy option providing both calcium and moderate protein.

6. Olives

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 2g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~20 (per 5 olives)

Ingredients:

  • Green or black olives

High in healthy fats and antioxidants, olives are a tasty salty snack.

7. Coconut Oil

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 14g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~120 (per tbsp)

Ingredients:

  • Pure coconut oil

Rich in MCTs, great for cooking or adding to keto coffee for extra fat.

8. Grass-Fed Butter

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 11g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~100 (per tbsp)

Ingredients:

  • Grass-fed butter

Provides a healthy dose of saturated fat, vitamins, and flavor.

9. Nuts (Almonds, Walnuts)

Carbs: 2-3g net | Fat: 14-18g | Protein: 4-6g | Calories: ~160-200 (per 1 oz)

Ingredients:

  • Almonds, walnuts, pecans, etc.

A crunchy snack source of healthy fats, fiber, and some protein. Watch portion sizes.

10. Seeds (Chia, Flax)

Carbs: 1-2g net | Fat: 8-9g | Protein: 4-5g | Calories: ~80-90 (per 2 tbsp)

Ingredients:

  • Chia seeds
  • Flax seeds

High in fiber and essential fatty acids, they're perfect for puddings and toppings.

11. Full-Fat Greek Yogurt

Carbs: ~4g net | Fat: 5g | Protein: 9g | Calories: ~120 (per 100g)

Ingredients:

  • Full-fat Greek yogurt

Creamy, tangy source of probiotics and moderate protein.

12. Cauliflower

Carbs: ~3g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 2g | Calories: ~25 (per cup)

Ingredients:

  • Fresh or frozen cauliflower florets

A versatile veggie used to make keto-friendly rice, mash, or pizza crust.

13. Zucchini

Carbs: ~3g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 1g | Calories: ~20 (per cup)

Ingredients:

  • Fresh zucchini

Great for making zucchini noodles, a low-carb pasta alternative.

14. Bell Peppers (Green, Red)

Carbs: 3-4g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 1g | Calories: ~30 (per 1/2 cup)

Ingredients:

  • Fresh bell peppers

Colorful, nutrient-rich, and add flavor without many carbs.

15. Berries (Strawberries, Raspberries)

Carbs: 5-6g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 1g | Calories: ~40-50 (per 1/2 cup)

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries, raspberries, blackberries

Low-glycemic fruits suitable in moderation for a sweet treat on keto.

16. Shirataki Noodles

Carbs: ~1g net | Fat: 0g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~10 (per serving)

Ingredients:

  • Shirataki (konjac) noodles

Made from konjac yam, very low in carbs, often used as a pasta replacement.

17. Meat & Poultry (Beef, Chicken, Pork)

Carbs: 0g | Fat & Protein Varies: ~150-250 Calories (per 3 oz)

Ingredients:

  • Choice of beef, chicken, or pork cuts

Staple proteins in a keto diet, providing essential amino acids and fats.

18. Bacon

Carbs: 0g | Fat: 9g | Protein: 3g | Calories: ~90 (per 2 slices)

Ingredients:

  • Bacon (preferably nitrate-free)

A popular high-fat, savory treat. Opt for nitrate-free if possible.

19. Heavy Cream

Carbs: ~1g | Fat: 5g | Protein: 0g | Calories: ~50 (per tbsp)

Ingredients:

  • Heavy whipping cream

Great for coffee, sauces, and adding extra fat to meet keto macros.

20. Sugar-Free Dark Chocolate

Carbs: ~3-5g net (depends on brand) | Fat: 10g | Protein: 2g | Calories: ~120 (per serving)

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate (85%+ cocoa, sugar-free sweetener)

An indulgent option in moderation if it's high-cocoa, low-sugar, and uses keto-friendly sweeteners.
