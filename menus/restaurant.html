Keto-Friendly Sit-Down Restaurant Meals

1. Outback Steakhouse – 6 oz Sirloin with Steamed Broccoli

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~15g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~300

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz sirloin
  • Steamed broccoli

Order the steak seasoned with salt & pepper, skip the potatoes, and add broccoli without cheese sauce.

2. Olive Garden – Herb-Grilled Salmon (No Pasta, Extra Veggies)

Carbs: ~6g | Fat: ~18g | Protein: ~35g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillet
  • Steamed or grilled veggies
  • No pasta

Substitute extra broccoli for pasta, and ask them to hold any high-carb sauces or breadsticks.

3. Chili’s – Bacon Avocado Grilled Chicken Sandwich (No Bun)

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~35g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken
  • Bacon, avocado
  • No bun

Remove the bun and fries, keep bacon & avocado. Add a side salad with low-carb dressing.

4. Applebee’s – 8 oz Top Sirloin with Garlicky Green Beans

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~370

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz sirloin steak
  • Green beans (garlicky)

Swap mashed potatoes for green beans or steamed broccoli. Skip high-carb sauces.

5. Red Lobster – Wood-Grilled Shrimp Skewers with Asparagus

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~10g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~220

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp skewers
  • Asparagus (or broccoli)
  • Minimal seasoning or sauces

Opt for asparagus instead of rice or biscuits. Watch out for sugary glazes or sauces.

6. TGI Fridays – Sizzling Chicken & Shrimp (No Potatoes, Extra Veg)

Carbs: ~8g | Fat: ~25g | Protein: ~30g | Calories: ~380

Ingredients:

  • Chicken & shrimp
  • Veggies instead of potatoes

Request double veggies instead of mashed potatoes. Go light on any sauce or glaze.

7. Buffalo Wild Wings – Traditional Wings (Dry Rub, Ranch Dip)

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~25g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~350 (10 wings)

Ingredients:

  • Traditional wings (bone-in)
  • Dry rub seasoning
  • Ranch or blue cheese dip

Stick to dry rubs and ranch or blue cheese dressing. Avoid sweet sauces.

8. P.F. Chang’s – Lettuce Wraps with Chicken (Less Sauce)

Carbs: ~7g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~20g | Calories: ~280

Ingredients:

  • Diced chicken
  • Lettuce cups
  • Minimal sauce

Ask for sauce on the side to control carbs. Use lettuce cups instead of rice.

9. Texas Roadhouse – Grilled Pork Chop with House Salad (No Croutons)

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~10g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~300

Ingredients:

  • Grilled pork chop
  • House salad (no croutons)
  • Oil & vinegar or ranch dressing

Order steamed veggies or side salad with ranch or oil & vinegar.

10. Carrabba’s – Chicken Bryan (Skip the Pasta, Extra Veggies)

Carbs: ~6g | Fat: ~18g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Goat cheese, sundried tomatoes
  • No pasta, extra veggies

Fresh mozzarella and sun-dried tomatoes on grilled chicken. Sub extra veggies for pasta sides.

11. Ruby Tuesday – "Create Your Own" Garden Bar Salad

Carbs: ~5-8g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~15-25g | Calories: ~300 (varies)

Ingredients:

  • Greens, low-carb toppings (cheese, bacon, eggs)
  • Keto-friendly dressing (ranch, oil & vinegar)

Choose greens, low-carb toppings, and a keto-friendly dressing like ranch or oil & vinegar.

12. Cheesecake Factory – SkinnyLicious Grilled Salmon

Carbs: ~6g | Fat: ~18g | Protein: ~35g | Calories: ~340

Ingredients:

  • Grilled salmon
  • Veggies (no starchy sides)

Swap out any carb-heavy side for grilled veggies. Portion sizes are large, so consider leftovers.

13. Maggiano’s – Chicken Piccata (No Pasta)

Carbs: ~7g | Fat: ~17g | Protein: ~30g | Calories: ~310

Ingredients:

  • Chicken cutlets
  • Lemon-butter sauce, capers
  • No pasta

Light lemon-butter sauce, capers, and mushrooms. Sub extra vegetables instead of pasta.

14. IHOP – Simple & Fit Omelette (Skip the Toast)

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~15g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~250

Ingredients:

  • Eggs, veggies
  • No toast or hash browns

Choose a veggie-filled omelette, skip hash browns and toast. Ask for extra veggies or bacon.

15. Denny’s – Build Your Own Grand Slam (Eggs, Bacon, Sausage)

Carbs: ~3-5g | Fat: ~25g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Bacon, sausage
  • No toast or pancakes

Opt for eggs, bacon, sausage, and skip pancakes or toast. Ask for a side of tomatoes or avocado if available.

16. LongHorn Steakhouse – 6 oz Renegade Sirloin with Caesar Salad (No Croutons)

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~360

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz sirloin
  • Caesar salad (no croutons)
  • No starchy sides

A classic steak dinner. Ask for a side of veggies instead of starch.

17. Cracker Barrel – Grilled Chicken Tenders with Green Beans

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~8g | Protein: ~30g | Calories: ~220

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken tenders
  • Green beans
  • No biscuit or gravy

Choose non-breaded chicken tenders, avoid biscuits, gravy, or sugary sauces.

18. Red Robin – Wedgie Burger (Bunless)

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~25g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Burger patty
  • Lettuce wrap
  • Bacon, guacamole

It’s a burger in a lettuce wrap with bacon, guacamole, and tomatoes. Avoid sugary sauces.

19. Perkins – Build Your Own Omelet

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~15g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~280

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Cheese, bacon, sausage, or veggies
  • No toast or hash browns

Load it up with cheese, low-carb veggies, and bacon or sausage. Hold the toast and hash browns.

20. BJ’s Brewhouse – Tri-Tip with Broccolini

Carbs: ~6g | Fat: ~25g | Protein: ~35g | Calories: ~380

Ingredients:

  • Tri-tip steak
  • Broccolini or Brussels sprouts
  • No starchy sides

Avoid the potato side. Ask for broccolini or Brussels sprouts. Skip sugary BBQ sauces.
