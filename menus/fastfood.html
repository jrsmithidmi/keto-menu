Keto Fast Food Options

1. McDonald's Bunless Double Cheeseburger

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~300

Ingredients:

  • 2 beef patties
  • Cheese slices
  • No bun
  • No ketchup

Order it without the bun and ketchup. Add lettuce, pickles, mustard, or mayo for extra flavor.

2. Burger King Bacon King (No Bun)

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~45g | Protein: ~40g | Calories: ~600

Ingredients:

  • 2 beef patties
  • Bacon strips
  • Cheese slices
  • No bun, no sugary sauce

Keep bacon and cheese but remove the bun and any sugary sauces. Ask for lettuce wrap if available.

3. Wendy's Grilled Chicken Sandwich (No Bun)

Carbs: ~2g | Fat: ~6g | Protein: ~29g | Calories: ~180

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • No bun
  • Optional lettuce, tomato

Skip the bun and high-sugar sauces; add a side salad with no dressing for a low-carb meal.

4. Chick-fil-A Grilled Chicken Club (No Bun)

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~9g | Protein: ~30g | Calories: ~220

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken
  • Bacon, cheese
  • No bun
  • Skip sauce or use low-carb options

Remove the bun, skip the sauce. Keep the bacon and cheese for extra fat.

5. In-N-Out "Protein Style" Burger

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~18g | Calories: ~250

Ingredients:

  • Beef patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce wrap
  • Optional tomato, onions

Lettuce-wrapped burger is a standard item here. Skip the spread or get it on the side.

6. Five Guys Bacon Cheeseburger (No Bun)

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~40g | Protein: ~35g | Calories: ~550

Ingredients:

  • 2 beef patties
  • Bacon, cheese
  • No bun
  • Optional lettuce wrap or bowl

Ask for a lettuce wrap or just place it in a bowl. Avoid ketchup or BBQ sauce to keep it keto.

7. Taco Bell Power Menu Bowl (No Rice, No Beans)

Carbs: ~7g | Fat: ~15g | Protein: ~20g | Calories: ~250

Ingredients:

  • Meat (beef or chicken)
  • Lettuce, cheese, sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • No rice, no beans

Order with extra lettuce, protein, cheese, and guacamole. Skip tortillas, beans, and rice.

8. KFC Grilled Chicken Breast

Carbs: ~0g | Fat: ~7g | Protein: ~38g | Calories: ~220

Ingredients:

  • KFC grilled chicken piece
  • No breading
  • Optional green beans

Stick to grilled options, and skip the breaded sides. Add green beans or coleslaw (watch the sugar!).

9. Subway Salad (Rotisserie-Style Chicken)

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~9g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~200

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Mixed lettuce and veggies
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Oil & vinegar dressing

Choose spinach or lettuce as the base, grilled chicken, cheese, veggies, and olive oil/vinegar.

10. Arby’s Roast Beef (No Bun)

Carbs: ~2g | Fat: ~10g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~200

Ingredients:

  • Roast beef slices
  • No bun
  • Minimal sauce

Opt out of the bun, sauce, and large sides. Arby’s has cheddar cheese sauce, but check the carbs first.

11. Shake Shack Lettuce-Wrapped ShackBurger

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~23g | Protein: ~24g | Calories: ~320

Ingredients:

  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce wrap
  • Optional tomato, ShackSauce on side

Request lettuce instead of a bun, skip the ShackSauce or get it on the side to control carbs.

12. Carl’s Jr. / Hardee’s Low Carb Thickburger

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~29g | Protein: ~28g | Calories: ~400

Ingredients:

  • Thickburger patty
  • Lettuce wrap
  • Cheese, pickles, onion (optional)
  • Omit ketchup

This item comes wrapped in lettuce. Omit ketchup for fewer carbs.

13. Popeyes Blackened Tenders

Carbs: ~2g | Fat: ~3g | Protein: ~26g | Calories: ~150 (3 tenders)

Ingredients:

  • Blackened seasoning
  • Chicken strips (no breading)

A unique low-carb option from Popeyes. Avoid biscuits and sugary sauces.

14. Culver’s No-Bun ButterBurger

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~23g | Protein: ~22g | Calories: ~320

Ingredients:

  • Single or double patty
  • No bun
  • Optional cheese, pickles, onion

Go for the single or double patty, hold the bun, and watch out for condiments with sugar.

15. Jimmy John’s Unwich

Carbs: ~3-5g | Fat: ~15g | Protein: ~20g | Calories: ~250

Ingredients:

  • Deli meat (turkey, ham, roast beef, etc.)
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce wrap
  • No high-sugar dressings

Their lettuce-wrapped sandwiches are widely known in keto circles. Skip sugary dressings or sauces.

16. Chipotle Salad Bowl (No Rice, No Beans)

Carbs: ~7g | Fat: ~20g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~300

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce base
  • Meat (chicken, beef, or pork)
  • Cheese, sour cream, guac
  • No rice, no beans

Base of lettuce, choice of meat, cheese, sour cream, guac, and mild salsa. Skip the high-carb extras.

17. Jack in the Box Grilled Chicken Salad

Carbs: ~5g | Fat: ~10g | Protein: ~25g | Calories: ~200

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken
  • Mixed lettuce & veggies
  • No croutons
  • Low-carb dressing if available

Order grilled chicken, avoid croutons, choose a low-carb dressing if available.

18. Del Taco Carne Asada Street Taco (No Tortilla)

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~10g | Protein: ~15g | Calories: ~170

Ingredients:

  • Carne asada
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato
  • No tortilla

Ask for the taco fillings in a bowl: steak, cheese, lettuce, and guac. Skip the tortilla.

19. White Castle Cheese Sliders (No Bun)

Carbs: ~3g | Fat: ~9g | Protein: ~8g | Calories: ~120 (per slider)

Ingredients:

  • Slider patties
  • Cheese
  • No bun

Order multiple sliders, remove buns, and watch the condiments. Avoid onion chips or fries.

20. Sonic Bacon Cheeseburger (No Bun)

Carbs: ~4g | Fat: ~27g | Protein: ~22g | Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • Beef patty
  • Bacon, cheese
  • No bun
  • Optional lettuce, tomato

Ask for a lettuce wrap or fork-and-knife style in a tray. Limit sugary sauces and skip sides.
