üõ°Ô∏è Understanding Augments

Augments determine shield compatibility and provide special perks. Choose based on mission type.

Shield Compatibility System

Light Shields

Protection: ~2-4 bars

Compatible with: Looting MK1, MK2, MK3

Best for: Loot runs, mobility

Medium Shields

Protection: ~4-6 bars

Requires: Raider Augment II or better

Best for: Balanced combat

Heavy Shields

Protection: 8+ bars

Requires: Combat Augments or top-tier

Best for: Heavy combat, PvP

Augment Types & Special Perks

Combat Augment Series

  • Combat Augment: Restores 1 HP every 5 seconds after taking damage (30s cooldown). Compatible with ALL shield types.
  • Best for: Survivability in extended fights

Looting Series - Mission Specialists

  • Looting MK1: Light shields only, basic starting augment
  • Looting MK2: 3 extra trinket slots = more money per run
  • Looting MK3 Survivor: 3 extra healing slots = maximum survivability
Mission Selection Guide:
  • üí∞ Money runs? ‚Üí Looting MK2
  • üõ°Ô∏è Survival/Combat? ‚Üí Looting MK3 or Combat Augment
  • ‚ö° PvP/Dangerous zones? ‚Üí Combat Augment with Heavy Shield
