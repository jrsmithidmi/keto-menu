Items: Rusted Gears, Motors, Light Bulbs, Mechanical Batteries

Route 1: Blue Gate Highway

  • Massive highway leading into tunnels (middle of map)
  • Flooded with cars, buses, and vehicles
  • Drops: Motors (needed for Refiner Level 3), Light Bulbs, Rusted Gears
  • Follow road all the way into the tunnels

Route 2: Buried City Car Park

  • Large concentration of vehicles
  • Drops: Rusted Gears, Motors

Route 3: Dam - Underground Primary Facility

  • Between Water Treatment and Control Tower
  • Shorter route but reliable
  • Drops: Rusted Gears, Mechanical Batteries
