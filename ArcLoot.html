# Arc Expedition Caravan - Complete Loot Location Guide ## Industrial Items (Rusted Gears, Motors, Light Bulbs) ### Route 1: Blue Gate Highway - Massive highway leading into tunnels (middle of map) - Flooded with cars, buses, and vehicles - **Drops:** Motors (needed for Refiner Level 3), Light Bulbs, Rusted Gears - Follow road all the way into the tunnels ### Route 2: Buried City Car Park - Large concentration of vehicles - **Drops:** Rusted Gears, Motors ### Route 3: Dam - Underground Primary Facility - Between Water Treatment and Control Tower - Shorter route but reliable - **Drops:** Rusted Gears, Mechanical Batteries --- ## Tech Items (Cooling Fans, Sensors) ### Small Building near Water Treatment (Dam) - Contains utility boxes - **Drops:** Sensors, Cooling Fans (needed for Expedition Project) ### Research Building (Dam) - Multiple utility boxes - **Drops:** Sensors, Cooling Fans ### Power Complex (Dam) - Described as "chill run" - **Drops:** Tech components --- ## Humidifiers (Stage 4 Expedition Caravan) ### Buried City - Red Tower (Best Spot - Guaranteed Spawn) - Top floor, two lockers - One locker always has a humidifier ### Santa Maria House (Buried City) - Enter from roof hatch - Frequently spawns humidifiers ### General Buried City - Random buildings and streets throughout the area --- ## Nature Items ### Apricots and Lemons (spawn in every map) - **Dam:** Islands in the Red Lakes - **Buried City:** Morirano Park - **Spaceport:** Staff Parking area - **Blue Gate:** Olive Grove (large quantity) ### Prickly Pears - **Spaceport:** Outskirts area (look for cactus clusters) - **Buried City:** Outskirts area (look for cactus clusters) ### Olives - **Blue Gate:** Olive Grove ### Mushrooms - **Spaceport:** Near little hangar on a specific tree (100% spawn) - **Dam:** Hallway near Water Treatment (100% spawn) - **Dam:** Throughout the swamps near trees ### All Nature Items (Best Method) **Lush Blooms Events** - Baskets spawn with stacks of up to 5 fruits - **Buried City - Morirano Park:** 3 known basket spawn locations - **Dam:** 6 basket locations (according to online map) - **Strategy:** Hit baskets and extract quickly for 3-4 efficient runs --- ## Medical Items (Laboratory Reagents, Bioscanners, Medical Kits) ### Buried City Hospital - Main location but heavily contested - **Drops:** Cracked Bioscanners, Rusted Shut Medical Kits, Laboratory Reagents ### Testing Annex (Dam) - Quieter alternative to hospital - Less loot but less competition - **Drops:** Same medical items as hospital --- ## Healing Items ### Bandages #### Galleria (Buried City) - 40-50 Great Mullen plants - Also counts for Harvesting Trail achievement #### Hospital next to Galleria - **Drops:** Fabric, Durable Cloth - **Crafting:** Fabric → Durable Cloth → Combine with Great Mullen = Herbal Bandages ### Shield Charges/Power Cells #### Husk Events (Dam) - Battlegrounds area when event is active - Search destroyed husks - **Drops:** Power Cells - **Warning:** High PvP activity #### Bastions and Bombarders (Farming Method) - **Drops:** Power Cells, Advanced Dark Power Cells (recycle into 2 normal cells) - Purple cells sell for 5k each - Good for both shields and money --- ## Money Farming Locations ### Spaceport - Departure Building - Room full of red lockers (rarely visited) - Check when nearby ### Dam - Research Building - Best at nighttime - Tons of desks and lockers ### Blue Gate Secret Tunnels - **Not recommended** (too well-known, high PvP) ### Key Rooms (All Maps) - Always worth it, especially at nighttime - Good loot even in common key rooms ### Late Game: Bombarder Spotters - Kill spotters near where Bombarders patrol - Each relay sells for ~5k - Spotters respawn --- ## Blueprints **No specific farming location** - spawn everywhere: - Caches - Desks - Lockers - Car hoods (especially on Blue Gate car route) --- ## Weapons ### Locked Rooms (All Maps) - Best at nighttime (better loot) - Often contain purple weapons ### PvP - High risk method - Recommended in squads rather than solo --- ## Always Pick Up (Priority Items) - Springs - Sensors - Mechanical Components - Electrical Components - Magnets - Gun Parts - Durable Cloth - Arc Circuitry - Power Cells - **Seeds** (stack to 100, convert to basic resources through Celeste) --- ## Pro Tips - Night runs generally have better loot - Solo lobbies are less competitive - During Lush Blooms events, prioritize basket farming for all nature items - Herbal Bandages are sufficient (Sterilized Bandages require too many resources) - Queen raid solo is not recommended (high risk, low reward, high ammo cost) - Always check car hoods on Blue Gate route for blueprints
